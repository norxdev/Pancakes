This is a tool to help players make profit in Old School RuneScape by flipping armour sets.

How to Flip Armour Sets

Buy individual armour pieces (like helm, platebody, legs, etc.) and combine them to create a full set for higher profit.

  1. Purchase: Acquire the individual pieces from the Grand Exchange at the prices listed below.
  2. Combine: Bring the pieces to a Grand Exchange Clerk to assemble them into a complete armour set.
  3. Sell: List the full set for sale on the Grand Exchange at the prices indicated below to maximize your profit.
