FlipMaster OSRS is a tool to help players make profit in Old School RuneScape through common flipping strategies.

  • Armor Flips → Buy individual armor pieces (like helm, platebody, legs, etc.) and resell them together as a full set for higher profit.
  • Potion Flips → Buy potions in 3-dose form, decant them into 4-dose, and sell at a markup.
